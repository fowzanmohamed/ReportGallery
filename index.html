Ask questions, get answers — no dashboards required

Fabric's conversational interface lets anyone in your business — not just analysts — explore data using plain English. It searches across every workspace, report, and semantic model you've granted access to, then returns direct answers with supporting visuals.

Cross-Workspace Discovery
Find any report or dataset across your entire Fabric environment in one search, regardless of which workspace it lives in.

Data Agents & Automation
Custom agents that act on your data without manual intervention

Data Agents are purpose-built for specific business workflows. We configure them to monitor your data, take defined actions when conditions are met, and connect with external tools — all without needing a developer to maintain the logic.

Business-Specific Configuration
Each agent is scoped to a specific use case — inventory alerts, revenue thresholds, SLA breaches — with logic tailored to your operations.
Direct Data Access
Agents connect directly to your Silver layer lakehouse or warehouse — bypassing the need for pre-built exports or scheduled refreshes.
External Tool Integration
Connect agents to Azure AI Foundry, Copilot Studio, Teams, or email — so actions happen where your team already works.
Proactive Stakeholder Alerts
Rather than waiting for someone to open a dashboard, agents push the right information to the right person the moment it matters.

Fabric Data Platform
The infrastructure that makes everything else possible

Before any report or AI capability can work, the data foundation needs to be right. Fabric's unified platform — Lakehouses, Warehouses, Pipelines, and OneLake — gives us a single place to build, manage, and govern everything.

OneLake — One Copy of Data
All data lives in a single logical lake. Every Fabric experience — reports, notebooks, agents — reads from the same source, eliminating duplicates and inconsistencies.
Medallion Architecture
We structure data through Bronze (raw), Silver (cleansed), and Gold (business-ready) layers — so the right level of data goes to the right consumer.
Real-Time & Batch Pipelines
Whether your data arrives in streams or daily batches, Fabric handles both — keeping reports and agents working from fresh, reliable data.
Unified Governance
Sensitivity labels, lineage tracking, and access controls are built in — not bolted on — so compliance and security are part of the architecture from day one.
